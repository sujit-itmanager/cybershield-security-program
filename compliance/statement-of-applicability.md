📜 Statement of Applicability (SoA)

SkySecure Pvt Ltd

1. Purpose

This Statement of Applicability defines the selection and justification of ISO/IEC 27001 controls based on the organization’s risk assessment and treatment process.

2. Scope

Applies to all systems and processes under the CyberShield Security Program.

3. Control Selection Criteria

Controls are selected based on:

Risk Register results

Regulatory requirements

Business objectives

Industry best practices

4. Control Applicability Table
Control Domain	Control Objective	Applicable (Yes/No)	Justification	Reference Document
Access Control	Restrict unauthorized access	Yes	High risk of unauthorized access	Access Control Standard
Information Classification	Protect sensitive data	Yes	Data breach risk	Data Classification Standard
Backup	Ensure recoverability	Yes	Ransomware risk	Backup Standard
Incident Management	Respond to incidents	Yes	Operational resilience	Incident Response Plan
Business Continuity	Maintain operations	Yes	Business impact	BCP
Cryptography	Encryption controls	Yes	Protect confidential data	Data Classification Std
5. Non-Applicable Controls

If any control is not applicable, justification must be documented here.

(Currently all major controls applicable.)

Reviewed annually
