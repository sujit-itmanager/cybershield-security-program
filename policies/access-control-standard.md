🔐 Access Control Standard

SkySecure Pvt Ltd

1. Purpose

This standard defines requirements for controlling access to information systems to ensure confidentiality, integrity, and availability of organizational data.

2. Scope

Applies to:

Employees

Contractors

Third-party users

All IT systems and cloud platforms

3. Access Control Principles

SkySecure follows:

Least Privilege

Need-to-Know

Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)

Segregation of Duties

Zero Trust principles

4. User Access Management

Unique user IDs required

Shared accounts prohibited

Access approval required from manager & system owner

Access revoked within 24 hours of exit

5. Privileged Access

Admin access limited and justified

MFA mandatory

Privileged activities logged and reviewed monthly

6. Authentication Requirements

MFA required for:

Email

VPN

Cloud systems

Administrative accounts

Password requirements:

Minimum 12 characters

Complexity enforced

Rotation every 90 days

7. Access Review

Quarterly access review

Privileged account review monthly

Dormant accounts disabled after 30 days

8. Remote Access

VPN required

Device compliance verification

Session timeout enforced

9. Monitoring & Logging

Login attempts logged

Failed login alerts monitored

Security team reviews anomalies

10. Compliance

Aligned with:

ISO 27001 Annex A – Access Control

Organizational Information Security Policy

11. Document Control
