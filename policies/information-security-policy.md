SkySecure Pvt Ltd

1. Purpose

This policy defines the framework for managing information security at SkySecure Pvt Ltd to protect organizational and client information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

2. Scope

This policy applies to:

All employees

Contractors

Vendors

Third-party partners

All IT systems and data assets

3. Objectives

Ensure confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information

Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

Minimize cybersecurity risks

Promote security awareness

Support business continuity

4. Roles & Responsibilities
Role	Responsibility
Board	Governance oversight
CEO	Strategic direction
CISO	Security leadership
IT Manager	Technical controls
Employees	Policy compliance
5. Information Security Principles

Least privilege access

Strong authentication

Secure configuration

Regular monitoring

Incident reporting

6. Access Control

Role-based access control (RBAC)

Multi-factor authentication (MFA)

Periodic access review

Immediate revocation on exit

7. Data Protection

Data classification

Encryption at rest and in transit

Secure backup

Secure disposal

8. Incident Management

All incidents must be reported within 1 hour

Incident Response Team (IRT) will handle events

Root cause analysis required

9. Compliance & Audit

Annual internal audit

External audit as required

Continuous improvement

10. Policy Review

This policy shall be reviewed annually or after major incidents.
