🚨 Incident Response Plan

SkySecure Pvt Ltd

1. Purpose

This document establishes the structured process for detecting, responding to, and recovering from cybersecurity incidents to minimize business impact and ensure timely resolution.

2. Scope

Applies to:

Security breaches

Malware or ransomware attacks

Unauthorized access

Data leakage

Insider threats

Cloud security incidents

3. Incident Classification
Severity	Description
Low	Minor policy violation
Medium	Limited system disruption
High	Significant operational impact
Critical	Major data breach or ransomware
4. Incident Response Team (IRT)
Role	Responsibility
CISO	Incident Commander
IT Manager	Technical Lead
SOC Team	Detection & Analysis
HR	Insider case handling
Legal	Regulatory communication
PR	Public communication
5. Incident Response Lifecycle

SkySecure follows a 6-step lifecycle:

Preparation

Identification

Containment

Eradication

Recovery

Lessons Learned

6. Escalation Matrix
Severity	Escalation
Low	IT Manager
Medium	CISO
High	CEO notified
Critical	Board + Legal notified
7. Communication Protocol

Internal notification within 1 hour

Executive update within 4 hours (High/Critical)

Regulatory notification as per legal requirement

Media handled by authorized spokesperson only

8. Evidence Handling

Preserve logs

Maintain chain of custody

Document all actions

Forensic analysis if required

9. Post-Incident Review

Root Cause Analysis

Control improvement

Policy update if needed

Report to Risk Committee

10. Testing & Drills

Annual tabletop exercise

Biannual ransomware simulation

Lessons documented
