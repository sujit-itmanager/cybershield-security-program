💻 Disaster Recovery Plan (DRP)

SkySecure Pvt Ltd

1. Purpose

This Disaster Recovery Plan defines procedures to restore IT systems and data following a disruptive cybersecurity incident or system failure to ensure minimal downtime and business impact.

2. Scope

Applies to:

Critical servers

Cloud infrastructure

Databases

Network services

Business applications

3. Recovery Objectives
Objective	Target
RTO (Recovery Time Objective)	8 hours
RPO (Recovery Point Objective)	24 hours
4. Disaster Scenarios

Ransomware attack

Data center outage

Cloud service failure

Major system compromise

Natural disaster affecting IT operations

5. DR Team Roles
Role	Responsibility
CISO	DR Coordinator
IT Manager	Recovery Lead
Infrastructure Team	System restoration
Security Team	Validation
Management	Business decisions
6. Recovery Process

Incident declared

Activate DR team

Assess damage

Restore from secure backup

Validate systems

Resume operations

Conduct post-recovery review

7. Recovery Prioritization
Priority	System
P1	Email & Identity Systems
P2	Client Database
P3	Business Applications
P4	Internal Tools
8. Communication Plan

Status updates every 2 hours during recovery

Executive briefing by CISO

Stakeholder notification via approved channels

9. Testing

Annual DR simulation

Backup restoration testing quarterly

Documentation updates after testing

10. Plan Maintenance

Reviewed annually

Updated after major incidents or infrastructure changes
