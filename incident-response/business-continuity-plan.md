🏢 Business Continuity Plan (BCP)

SkySecure Pvt Ltd

1. Purpose

This Business Continuity Plan ensures that critical business operations continue during and after disruptive incidents, including cyberattacks, infrastructure failures, or natural disasters.

2. Scope

Applies to:

Core business functions

IT operations

Customer services

Finance and HR operations

3. Business Impact Analysis (BIA)
Business Function	Impact if Disrupted	Maximum Downtime
Client Services	High	8 hours
Email & Identity	Critical	4 hours
Finance Systems	High	24 hours
HR Operations	Medium	48 hours
4. Continuity Strategies

Remote work enablement

Alternate cloud hosting region

Manual process fallback

Backup communication channels

5. Roles & Responsibilities
Role	Responsibility
CEO	Overall business decisions
CISO	Security coordination
IT Manager	Technical recovery
Department Heads	Business continuity execution
HR	Staff communication
6. Communication Plan

Incident notification to management within 1 hour

Client communication approved by leadership

Crisis communication handled by authorized spokesperson

7. Alternate Work Arrangements

Remote work infrastructure

VPN capacity planning

Secondary communication channels

8. Testing & Exercises

Annual BCP drill

Tabletop simulation exercises

Improvement actions documented

9. Review & Maintenance

Reviewed annually

Updated after major incidents

Integrated with DR and Incident Response Plan
