1. **Purpose**

This document defines the cybersecurity governance structure at SkySecure Pvt Ltd to ensure effective oversight, accountability, and strategic alignment of information security with business objectives.

2. **Scope**

This governance structure applies to:

Board of Directors

Executive Management

IT & Security Teams

All employees and contractors

Third-party partners

3. Governance Objectives

Establish clear accountability for cybersecurity

Ensure regulatory compliance

Manage organizational risk

Support business continuity

Enable informed decision-making

5.  **Roles & Responsibilities**
Role	                   Key Responsibilities

IT Manager             Implements controls
SOC Team	             Monitors threats

6. **Committees & Working Groups**
6.1 Information Security Steering Committee (ISSC)

Members: CEO, CISO, IT Manager, HR Head, Legal Head
Meetings: Quarterly

  Responsibilities:

  Review risk posture

  Approve major initiatives

  Monitor compliance

  Review incidents

6.2 Risk Management Committee

Members: CISO, Finance Head, Operations Head
Meetings: Monthly

Responsibilities:

Review enterprise risks

Approve treatment plans

Monitor KRIs
