📊 Risk Treatment Plan

SkySecure Pvt Ltd

1. Purpose

This document defines the treatment strategy for identified information security risks at SkySecure Pvt Ltd in accordance with the Risk Management Framework.

2. Treatment Strategy

SkySecure adopts the following treatment options:

Mitigate – Implement controls to reduce risk

Avoid – Eliminate the activity causing risk

Transfer – Share risk via insurance or vendors

Accept – Formally acknowledge residual risk
