📊 Risk Management Framework

SkySecure Pvt Ltd

1. Purpose

This document defines the enterprise risk management framework for identifying, assessing, treating, monitoring, and reviewing information security risks at SkySecure Pvt Ltd.

This framework aligns with:

ISO/IEC 27001:2022

ISO 31000 Risk Management Principles

CISM Risk Management Practices

2. Scope

This framework applies to:

All business processes

IT systems and infrastructure

Cloud environments

Third-party services

Employees and contractors

3. Risk Management Objectives

Protect confidentiality, integrity, and availability (CIA)

Reduce likelihood and impact of cyber incidents

Support regulatory compliance

Enable informed management decisions

Align risk exposure with business risk appetite

4. Risk Governance Structure
Role	Responsibility
Board	Approves risk appetite
CEO	Ensures risk alignment with business
CISO	Owns risk framework
IT Manager	Implements controls
Risk Owners	Manage assigned risks
Internal Audit	Independent review
5. Risk Management Process

SkySecure follows a structured lifecycle:

Step 1: Risk Identification

Asset identification

Threat identification

Vulnerability assessment

Historical incident analysis

Step 2: Risk Analysis

Risk is calculated as:

Risk Score = Impact × Likelihood

Impact and likelihood are rated on a scale of 1–5.

6. Risk Rating Criteria
6.1 Impact Scale
Rating	Description
1	Negligible
2	Minor
3	Moderate
4	Major
5	Critical

Impact considers:

Financial loss

Regulatory penalties

Reputational damage

Operational disruption

6.2 Likelihood Scale
Rating	Description
1	Rare
2	Unlikely
3	Possible
4	Likely
5	Almost Certain
6.3 Risk Levels
Score	Level	Action Required
1–5	Low	Monitor
6–10	Medium	Mitigate
11–15	High	Immediate Treatment
16–25	Critical	Executive Escalation
7. Risk Treatment Options

SkySecure adopts one of the following strategies:

Risk Avoidance

Risk Mitigation

Risk Transfer

Risk Acceptance

All accepted risks must be formally approved by the CISO and CEO.

8. Risk Appetite

SkySecure maintains:

Low tolerance for regulatory and data breach risks

Medium tolerance for operational disruption

Zero tolerance for intentional insider fraud

Risk appetite is reviewed annually.

9. Risk Monitoring & Review

Monthly review by Risk Committee

Quarterly review by ISSC

Annual independent audit

Continuous monitoring via SOC

10. Documentation & Reporting

The following documents support this framework:

Risk Register

Risk Treatment Plan

Incident Reports

Audit Findings

KPI/KRI Dashboard

11. Continuous Improvement

The framework is reviewed annually or after major incidents to ensure effectiveness and alignment with evolving threats.
